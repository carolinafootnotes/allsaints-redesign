Worship & Music

Serve in worship

Grades 3-12

Acolytes

Young people who serve at the altar, carrying the cross and torches, and assisting clergy during worship services.

Lectors, Chalice Bearers & Intercessors

Read Scripture aloud, lead the Prayers of the People, and share communion with the congregation during services.

Altar Guild

Lovingly prepare the sanctuary each week, arranging linens, flowers, candles, and communion elements for worship.

Choir of All Saints

Our parish choir rehearses Wednesdays at 7 PM and sings at the 11:10 AM Sunday service. All voices are welcome.

Resurrection Choir

A contemporary music group that leads worship at the 9 AM service with a blend of traditional and modern hymns.

Bells of All Saints

Our handbell ensemble rehearses Wednesdays at 6 PM, adding a beautiful voice to worship throughout the liturgical year.

Youth

St. Nicholas Choir & Junior Choir

St. Nicholas Choir (grades 1-3) and Junior Choir (grades 4-7) nurture young voices and a love of sacred music.

Interested in music ministry? Contact Brian Sapp-Moore to learn more.

Love your neighbor in action

Stephen Ministry

Trained lay caregivers who walk alongside people during difficult times, offering confidential one-to-one Christian care.

Pastoral Care

Support through life's challenges, including hospital visits, home communion, prayer shawls, and caring presence during times of need.

Koinonia

Community fellowship and service in the spirit of early Christian sharing. Building bonds of friendship through meals, gatherings, and outreach.

CCM Volunteer Opportunities

Partner with Cooperative Christian Ministry to serve our neighbors through food assistance, crisis support, and community programs.

Learn & Grow

Education for all ages

Sunday School

Classes for children and adults between services, exploring the Bible and our faith through engaging, age-appropriate lessons.

Bible Study

Deepen your understanding of Scripture in a welcoming small-group setting with conversation, context, and prayer.

Youth

Youth Group (EYC)

Episcopal Youth Community for middle and high school students. Fellowship, service projects, and growing in faith together.

Our Clergy & Staff

Meet the people who lead us

Rev. John Johanssen

Interim Rector

A native of Ohio, Rev. Johanssen holds an M.Div. from Virginia Theological Seminary and has specialized in interim ministry, guiding congregations through seasons of transition. He enjoys music, sailing, and hiking.

Rev. Jim Bernacki

Priest Associate

A graduate of Canisius College with an M.Div. from Christ the King Seminary, Rev. Bernacki coordinates Tuesday Compline and brings a warm pastoral presence. He is married to Sandy.

Rev. Mary Elizabeth Reese

Priest Associate

A Cornell graduate with an M.Div. from Virginia Theological Seminary, Rev. Reese previously served in Sierra Leone. She brings deep global perspective to her ministry and is the proud grandmother of 11 grandchildren.

Rev. Vern Cahoon

Deacon

With degrees from UMass Dartmouth and Northeastern Law, Rev. Cahoon brings a unique background to his diaconal ministry. His focus is pastoral care for nursing home residents. He is married to Lori.

Children & Families

Lockhart Child Development Center

Our church-affiliated childcare center has been serving the Concord community for decades, providing a nurturing, safe, and enriching environment where young children learn, play, and grow. Lockhart CDC offers programs for infants through pre-kindergarten.
