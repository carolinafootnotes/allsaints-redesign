New Here?

Come as you are. Stay as family.

Whether you are visiting from afar or have lived in Concord your whole life, you are welcome here. We are a faith community where God's love meets you exactly where you are.

Our 9 AM contemporary service is casual and family-friendly. Our 11:10 AM traditional service features choir and organ. Both are livestreamed. Nursery available for infants and toddlers. Accessible entrances on all sides.
